How It All Started

My journey into programming started with curiosity. What began as learning Java evolved into building games, management systems, desktop applications, and eventually websites.

As I continued learning, I realized I also enjoyed explaining technical concepts. Writing tutorials and documenting projects became just as rewarding as building them.
